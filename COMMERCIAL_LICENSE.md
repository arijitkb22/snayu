## The AGPL Explained Simply

The AGPL-3.0 has one key rule beyond the standard GPL:

> If you run a **modified version** of Snayu over a **network** (as a service), you must make your modifications available as open source to the users of that service.

This prevents companies from taking Snayu, adding proprietary features, and offering it as a competing cloud service without contributing back — while still allowing everyone else to use it freely.
